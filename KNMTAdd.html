ADMINISTRATIVE SANCTION

Introduction

The Greater Chennai Corporation (GCC) has an area of 426 Sq Km and a population of 66.72 lakh as per the 2011 census. The boundaries of Greater Chennai Corporation were extended by including 42 adjacent Local Bodies in the year 2011. Consequently, the area of the Greater Chennai Corporation increased from 174 sq.km. to 426 sq.km. There are gaps in the basic infrastructure in these added areas, which require special attention. Therefore, with a view to improve basic infrastructure in the Corporation, the Government has decided to launch ‘Singara Chennai 2.0’ programme. The aim of 'Singara Chennai 2.0’ is to provide modern world-class infrastructure and services in Greater Chennai Corporation with a special focus towards added areas.
